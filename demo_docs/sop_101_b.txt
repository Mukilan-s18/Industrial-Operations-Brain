PUMP MAINTENANCE QUICK REFERENCE (SOP-101-B)
Revision: 2

This is a quick reference guide for all plant pumps including P-101.

1. Maintenance
- Power off and LOTO (Lockout/Tagout).
- Remove housing bolts.
- Check impeller.

2. Torque Specifications
Important: For P-101, all housing bolts must be tightened to a torque of 30 Nm. Do not over-tighten as it will warp the casing.

3. Valve Limits
Valve HV-204 should never exceed 150 PSI.
