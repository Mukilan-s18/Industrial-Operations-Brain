WORK ORDER: WO-2025-998
Asset: Pump P-101
Date: 2025-11-04
Technician: John Doe

Issue Description:
Operator reported that Pump P-101 is leaking fluid from the main casing seal.

Resolution:
Inspected the pump and found the casing bolts were loose. The torque was measured at 20 Nm.
Re-torqued all housing bolts to the specified 45 Nm. Replaced the primary O-ring seal which had minor wear.
Pump tested and confirmed no longer leaking.

Notes:
Suspect vibration from adjacent equipment might be loosening the bolts over time.
