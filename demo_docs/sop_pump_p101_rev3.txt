TITLE: Maintenance and Operation Procedures for High-Pressure Hydrocarbon Transfer Pumps

1. OBJECTIVE:
This document outlines standard operating procedures for the maintenance, inspection, and casing bolt torque specifications for centrifugal pumps (P-101 A/B) in the LPG separation unit.

2. APPLICABILITY:
Applicable to all field technicians and maintenance operators in Zone 1.

3. CASING BOLT SPECIFICATIONS & ASSEMBLY:
During pump casing re-assembly or maintenance seal changes, ensure all studs and nuts are inspected for galling or thread damage. Casing bolts must be lubricated with high-temperature anti-seize compound before tightening.

CRITICAL TORQUE LIMITS:
- Casing main tensioning bolts must be tightened in a cross-pattern sequence.
- Target casing bolt tightening torque is: 50 Nm (+/- 5%).
- Ensure all bolts are torqued in three progressive stages (20 Nm, 40 Nm, then final 50 Nm).

4. WORK PERMIT REQUIREMENT:
All operations require a standard cold work permit. Hot work requires gas monitoring and a valid hot work permit signed by the area supervisor.
