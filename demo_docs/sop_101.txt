PUMP P-101 MAINTENANCE PROCEDURE (SOP-101)
Revision: 4

1. Purpose
This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the steps for routine maintenance of Pump P-101.

2. Safety Warnings
- Ensure all power is disconnected before servicing.
- Wear appropriate PPE (gloves, safety glasses).

3. Procedure Steps
3.1. Isolate the pump by closing inlet valve HV-204 and outlet valve HV-205.
3.2. Drain the casing completely.
3.3. Remove the housing bolts using a cross-pattern.
3.4. Inspect the impeller for wear or cavitation damage. Replace if tolerance exceeds 0.5mm.
3.5. Reassemble the housing.

4. Torque Specifications
When reassembling, all housing bolts must be tightened to a torque of 45 Nm.

5. Valve Pressure Limits
The maximum allowable operating pressure for valve HV-204 is 120 PSI. Exceeding this limit may cause seal failure.
